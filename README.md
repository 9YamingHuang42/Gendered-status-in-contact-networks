# Gendered-status-in-contact-networks
Focused on frequent-contact social relationships, we explored gender difference in positions in social networks across three key socio-demographic factors: age and post-marital residence pattern and kinship system, as well as gender-specific effects of participarion in market activities and reputation acquisition on network positions.
